What Can You Make?


How many times have you stared into your pantry and thought, "What am I gonna make for dinner tonight?" At TopShelf, we want to help with that. Simply create an account, tell us what ingredients you have in your pantry, and we'll give you a list of great recipes that you can make with what you already have. Also, once you've created an account, you can add new recipes, keep track of your current ingredients, and even make a shopping list of what ingredients you might need to make a certain recipe. So instead of spending another afternoon staring into your pantry, let us help you see what you can make with what you already have.
